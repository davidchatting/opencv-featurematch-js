opencv-featurematch: plain DOM demo

No p5.js, no canvas rendering - just two <img> elements and a direct call to alignImagePair().

These are the classic OpenCV tutorial images, photographed at a real perspective angle rather than the near-planar bursts isReasonableHomography's defaults were tuned for - so this demo relaxes maxPerspective to show the thresholds are configurable per call.
