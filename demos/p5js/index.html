opencv-featurematch: p5.js demo

Loads two images, aligns them once with alignImagePair(), then uses the resulting transform to rectify the scene photo so the box appears head-on (left), drawn with drawProjectedImage() - both from imgproc.js. The original reference image (right) is shown for comparison. No EXIF, camera fly-through, or playback timeline (see the RugbySynth app for that).
